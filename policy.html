Site Purpose & Accessibility

Site Purpose Statement

The purpose of this website is to connect military veterans with clear, trustworthy information about housing, employment, mental health, and benefits resources.

Accessibility Statement

We are committed to making this website accessible to all users, including veterans with disabilities.

  • High color contrast
  • Readable font sizes
  • Descriptive alt text
  • Keyboard-accessible navigation
  • Responsive layouts
